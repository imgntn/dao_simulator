\begin{itemize}

    \item Buyback caps stabilize token price but limit treasury's ability to respond to market opportunities
    \item Shock resilience depends on the interaction between buffer size and spending rate
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Inter-DAO Cooperation (RQ5)}

Cross-DAO resource-sharing introduces coordination benefits and risks:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Homogeneous DAO pairs cooperate more readily but offer fewer complementary resources
    \item Heterogeneous pairs unlock greater value but face alignment challenges
    \item Asymmetric partnerships risk exploitation of smaller DAOs by larger ones
\end{enumerate}

Joint-venture proposals succeed when governance alignment exceeds a threshold that depends on the relative scale and resource profiles of participating DAOs.

\begin{enumerate}
