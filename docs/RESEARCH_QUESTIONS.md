# Candidate Research Questions (Pick 3-5)

Selected top 5 (locked):
1) Participation dynamics: How do participation incentives and delegation policies affect turnout, quorum reach, and decision quality?
2) Governance capture: Do voting power mitigations (caps, quadratic thresholds, velocity penalties) reduce capture without harming throughput?
3) Proposal pipeline: How does a temp-check -> on-chain pipeline change pass rates, time-to-decision, and abandonment?
4) Treasury resilience: How do treasury buffers and buyback caps impact treasury volatility and long-run sustainability under shocks?
5) Inter-DAO cooperation: Under what conditions do cross-DAO resource-sharing or joint-venture proposals improve welfare vs increase conflict?
